About Us


Artificial Intelligence And Robotics Club: We aim at building a community of dynamic people to gain expertise ,showcase their skills in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics.Providing students the rights tools,guidance to experiment and excel in the Field of AI and Robotics is our motive.We are a community to solve problems,build things and make things easier and simpler.

Events


Retina for computers-A workshop for beginners in computer vision,covering introduction to computer vision ,opencv library ,mediapipe framework with interative project using mediapipe.

Nvidia Deep Learning workshop- A deep learning workshop hosted every year,a part of nvidia deep learning institute program .The workshop covers fundamental of deep learning to transfer learning with interactive notebooks and assignments.

Fundamentals of python bootcamp- A 6 week complete bootcamp on python,covering basics of python to getting started with dataScience libraries. The workshop consisted elaborate sessions on fundamentals of python ,oops and data science libraries and a project to provide hands on expeience.

Arduino expo-A beginner friendly arduino workshop to help the robotic enthusiasts get started.The event covered basics of arduino and sensors with interactive experiments.

Kinova Gen 3 robotics seminar- A seminar on robotics by Mr.Aditya Marathe ,the founder of Nugenux Robotics.
